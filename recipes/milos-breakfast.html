Milo's Nutritious and Ravishing Breakfast

You should only feel bad about this one if you're not a dog.


Ingredients

  • Dog food
  • Dog treats
  • Dog bowl
  • Dog

Steps

  1. Tell Milo (the dog) it is his dinner time. Expect excitement.
  2. Pour the dog food into the dog bowl. Shake it around a little to induce further excitement. Caution: do not let any escape over the sides!
  3. Add the dog treats, they act like seasonings for Milo and he may refuse plain food without the incentive. Break large treats into smaller bits.
  4. Place the bowl on the ground and when Milo is sitting patiently, tell him to "ATTACK!".
